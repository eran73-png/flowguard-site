How it works

1
Download the tool
Click the download button above. Save it anywhere (Desktop is fine).
2
Run it (double-click)
A console window will open and the tool will collect diagnostic information. This takes 5-15 seconds.
3
Your email client opens
The tool creates a sanitized ZIP file on your Desktop and opens your default email client with everything pre-filled — ready to send to support.
4
Drag the ZIP into the email
A File Explorer window opens with the ZIP selected. Drag it into the email, add a short description of the issue, and click Send.
5
We'll get back to you
Eran personally reviews every report and responds within 24 hours with a solution.
🔒 Your privacy is protected
  • Tokens, passwords, and API keys are automatically redacted
  • Your username is replaced with [USER] in all paths and logs
  • Telegram bot tokens are removed before packaging
  • All sanitization happens locally — no data leaves your computer until you click Send
  • The ZIP file is saved to your Desktop so you can review it before sending
